Brand and customize your marketplace

VQ Marketplace comes with intuitive settings so you can quickly and easily customize every facet of your storefront in the admin dashboard. In premium edition of VQ Marketplace, we do it for you.

Unlimited products

There's no limit to the number or type of products you are presented in your online marketplace.

Various business cases covered

VQ Marketplace supports the following business cases: product, services, rental and contingent sales.

Integration with your CAS

We offer integration with your CAS providers, such as Genesis World or SAP. This way you can combine VQ-Marketplace and just use a subset of the offered features.

Unlimited bandwidth

You'll never be charged based on the number of visitors or traffic to your store.

Daily backups

All your business information, including products, customers, orders, and inventory, is backed up in our secure data centers every day.

Dedicated hardware

Your data is physically separated from other. The hardware setup is dedicated for you marketplace.

Instant upgrades

All VQ updates are automatic, so you'll receive the latest features immediately, without any hassle.

99.5% uptime and 24/7 monitoring

No need to worry about downtime with VQ-Marketplace, we work around the clock to make sure your website is always online.

Google Analytics

Google Analytics works with your online store, so you can track sales, visits, and referrals.

Dashboard

An actionable dashboard of your sales, orders, and traffic helps you make the right choices for your business.

Customer profiles

Learn more about your customers and their shopping habits. Find their contact info and order history at a glance.

Multiple languages

Your online marketplace checkout comes in internationalisation module and you can always translate your marketplace's theme to suit your needs.
