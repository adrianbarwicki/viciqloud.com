Marketplace AI

VQ Marketplace AI learns your users' behaviour and recommends personalized products to your users, or vice versa.

Smart Marketplace

We integrate Artifical Intelligence / Machine Learning algorithms to boost your business.

Recommended items for users


You can use VQ-Marketplace AI to display recommended items for your users. Displaying recommended items can help tremedously in user engagement and retention.

Recommended users for products


If you've a new item or a group of items or a campaign, you can use VQ Marketplace AI to target users for these items. This way you can present interesting content to some of your users while not bothering the rest of them.

Find similiar users

Being able to find which users are similar to each other could benefit your users significantly when they are able to find other likeminded users. Personal connections made in your platform would help you with increasing loyalty.

Find similiar products

You may promote similar items on item pages for your users. Since they're already visiting an item, they could be more likely to see other items that similar users also visit. You can also use this feature to predict tags or categories for your items.

How it works?

Once set up, interaction of your users with recommendations will lead to more user engagement, better customer retention, and more revenue. It will only get better with time as VQ Marketplace AI understands your application's patterns better.

Architecture

VQ Marketplace AI’s application architecture wraps high-performance machine learning and prediction into an innovative, fault-tolerant modern technology stack. The application works in a public cloud using containers for secure and private front-end, computation and internal storage. The enterprise edition is extensible to virtual private clouds and parallelized compute infrastructures.
