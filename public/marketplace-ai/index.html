Recommended users for products


If you've a new item or a group of items or a campaign, you can use VQ Marketplace AI to target users for these items. This way you can present interesting content to some of your users while not bothering the rest of them.

Find similiar users

Being able to find which users are similar to each other could benefit your users significantly when they are able to find other likeminded users. Personal connections made in your platform would help you with increasing loyalty.
