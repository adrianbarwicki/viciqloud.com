We're hiring

Our tech stack is mostly built with Scala, NodeJS and Python. We use AWS for hosting. Our frontend solution currently use JavaScript, ReactJS and Angular. We do a lot of data science and machine learning. VQ-LABS is a small but fast growing company building very unique technology from the ground up. We engineer almost everything in our product, right down to the bits and bytes. We don't fill roles, we add smart people with passion.

Senior Developer - Machine Learning Expert (EN & DE)

Required

  • Degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Physics, Statistics or related work experience
  • Proficiency in handling of multi-terabyte datasets in distributed Linux/Unix environments
  • Ability to visualize data and present core insights in a clear and compelling way
  • Excellent written and communication skills in English and German language
  • Experience with JavaScript
  • Knowledge about distributed computing
  • Experience with Machine / Deep Learning software packages & libraries such as Caffe, Torch, Theano, TensorFlow, Scikit-learn, DeepLearning4j etc; programming languages such as Python, C++, Java, Scala etc.
  • Experience with Cassandra
  • Deep knowledge about Machine learning, especially Recommendation Algorithms

Optional

  • PhD degree in Computer Science, Mathematics, Statistics, Operations Research or related field

Marketing & Communications (DE&EN)
