We are hiring

Our tech stack is mostly built with Scala, NodeJS and Python. We use AWS for hosting. Our frontend solution currently use JavaScript, ReactJS and Angular. We do a lot of data science and machine learning. VQ-LABS is a small but fast growing company building very unique technology from the ground up. We engineer almost everything in our product, right down to the bits and bytes. We don't fill roles, we add smart people with passion.
Drop your CV and github (only for IT-related positions) at ani.calis[@]viciqloud.com. If you do not find any suitable position for you and you think that you are the best in your area, apply anyway. We might find something for you.
