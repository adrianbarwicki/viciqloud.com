Sharing Economy Usecases

VQ Labs offers a custom solution for each type of marketplace.

C2C Marketplace:

Customer-to-customer marketplace or known by many as peer-to-peer marketplace is one of the most popular types of marketplaces in recent years.
In C2C marketplaces, two sides interact to buy or sell goods or services.
Both buyers and sellers transact directly with each other without any intermediation of a third party.

If you would like to have your own C2C customized marketplace like Airbnb, TaskRabbit, Uber or anything else, contact us and let's start building your own customized C2C marketplace.

B2C Marketplace:

Business-to-customer marketplaces are mostly e-commerce platforms where the sellers can be any type of business while the buyers are individuals. In recent years, the number of B2C marketplaces have increased since several big brands have started their e-commerce platforms as well as small business, who try to get benefit of blessings of the internet age.

Either you already have an offline business and would like to go online, or you have brand new idea of B2C marketplace, we are here to help you. Contact us.

B2B Marketplace:

Business-to-business marketplaces are e-commerce platforms where both buyers and sellers are wide range of suppliers, manufacturers and retailers.
B2B marketplaces differ from the other type of marketplaces in terms of payment method and ordering process.

Contact us now and have the technology covered for your B2B marketplace.
