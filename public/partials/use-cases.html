VQ Labs supports varied sharing economy usecases and enables you to have your own online marketplace that fits into your business model as well as your industry.

Buy & Sell goods/services

An online marketplace where users can buy and sell products or services. The use case is mostly seen either as an eCommerce platform or on-demand service platform.


Rentals & Bookings

An online marketplace where users can rent or book spaces, goods, services or events. The examples of use case can be seen as C2C, B2C or B2B Marketplace platform.


Knowledge & Talent sharing

An online platform where users share their knowledge and skills. The use case can be adapted to each marketplace type, also can be used as an internal solution in an enterprise.
