Marketplace Features

We want to empower non-technical entepreneurs to start their own marketplaces in the field of Sharing Economy.

@@include('../partials/features-essentials.html')

Marketplace Types

You need a marketplace where your users could both can sell/offer and book/buy? services from one another? We make it available.

Facebook Login

You can allow your users to either signup with their email address, or with their facebook account.

Different User Types

As a marketplace owner, you will be able to configure the user types of your marketplace. E.g. you can restrict the users' role to one (supply or demand) or make them have both roles at the same time.

Browsing

Allow your users to browse, filter and search items. Your users can browse listings as a list, grid or map. It is possible to filter items based on their location, category name and price.

Booking Calendar

VQ-Marketplace provides you with booking calendar that will make your users specify the listing's availability, which is shown to users in the listing's calendar.

Profiles

The users have their own verified profile page with their preferences, short biography, picture, and ratings and reviews.

Booking Scenarios

We support different booking scenarios for your Marketplace. Listings can have many requests and many bookings, many requests but one booking, or one request and one booking.

Easy to Monetize

As a marketplace owner, you will be able to set up the payment provider just in few minutes. VQ-Marketplace supports Stripe as a payment provider and has a built-in payment system

Ratings & Review

The users will be able to rate and leave reviews to the listings.

Chat

Your users can connect with each other through chat. The users will have their own inbox inside the marketplace, which helps them keep track of their orders.

Email notifications

You will be able to send automated emails to your users. You can easily configure the content of each email through Admin dashboard.

Pricing scenarios

VQ-Marketplace allows you to set up different pricing scenarios based on your business model. Your users will be able to use one of the following pricing scnarios in their listings. Price per hour, price per listing or price on request.

Admin Dashboard

Easily configure and manage your marketplace from admin dashboard.

Analytics

You can integrate Google Analytics into your marketplace through admin dashboard and have a detailed view of what is happening in your platform and optimize it accordingly.

Custom Pages

As a marketplace owner, you will be able to integrate custom pages or websites to your marketplace. It can be a blog, a contact page or anything else that you want to connect to your marketplace.<
