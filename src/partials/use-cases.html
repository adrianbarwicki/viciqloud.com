Marketplaces in Sharing Economy

Online marketplaces have been popular recently with the riso of "Sharing Economy", which allows people to easily access to products and services from someone else through an internet platform on a peer-to-peer (C2C), B2C and B2B basis. VQ Labs supports varied sharing economy use cases and enables you to have your own online marketplace that fits into your business model as well as your industry.
