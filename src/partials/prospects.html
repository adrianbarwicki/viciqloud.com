Decentralized Marketplace Network

The next step is to introduce a element of decentralization to the system. We introduce an architecture of global decentralized database of listings, transactions, disputes and more. Multiple rentals-, services- and ride-sharing marketplaces can connect to this global database. Those platforms become in this context just “enablers” or “storefronts” of the transactions between the demand and supply side. We develop decentralized protocols for storage of listings, for transactions for sharing economy use cases and resolving disputes in the ecosystem.
