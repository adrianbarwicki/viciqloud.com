Buy & Sell goods/services

The marketplace use case is one of the most popular usecases in recent years since several big brands have started their own marketplace platforms.The usecase is mostly seen in eCommerce and on-demand business where two sides interact to buy or sell goods or services.Both buyers and sellers transact directly with each other without any intermediation of a third party.
Some of the most noteworthy examples of this use case are Etsy, Alibaba and TaskRabbit.

Rentals & Bookings

In recent years, rental and booking marketplace platforms are seen as an important example of peer to peer marketplaces thanks to Airbnb and Blablabla. In such marketplaces users interact to rent or book spaces, goods, services or events.
Differently than the previous usecase, rental and booking marketplaces require more features such as calendar, geolocation, filtering and so on.

Knowledge & Talent sharing

In knowledge and talent sharing platforms users offer their skills, specific services and knowledge to other users. Differently than previous two use cases, knowledge and talent sharing platforms are adopted by enterprises for their internal affairs. The use case is used in several corporates in order to increase the employees' motivation and the engagement to company culture.
An example for this specific case would be a a platform where employees would offer their interests and skills they expertise and would find or match accordingly to the projects in the company.
